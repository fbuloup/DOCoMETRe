ADS1115 Module

ADS1115 module is a ΔΣ DAC with 16 bits resolution and four channels. With DOCoMETRe, it's possible to acquire one channel at 600Hz in continuous mode. In single-shot mode, one can acquire at 300Hz per channel. If you need more details on this module, please follow the first two links below. The other links point to messages in Texas Instrument forum related to acquisition time.
